Support Funnels are mechanisms by which you can collect feedback or issues from your customers. You can choose the means of customer engagement on your website or on the mobile device.

Once you decide on the funnels, you will get a simple Javascript code to install on your website. Our mobile app automatically picks your latest funnel configuration.

Funnels are meant to provide an array of tools to let your customers reach you. The issues/feedback collected via the funnels you setup are reported on the Dashboard.
