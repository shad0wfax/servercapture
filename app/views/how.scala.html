Sign up!

When you sign up, you will be taken to the Dashboard page.
You will be automatically assigned an "API KEY" (located in the Administration tab of Dashboard) and can perform the following tasks to get started:

  • Create a New Funnel
  • Install Funnel Javascript (for Web Engagement only)
  • Add Services (optional)
  • Set up Notifcations, Rules, and other settings

Create A Funnel

  • To get started you have to create a Funnel for your website / service.
  • Funnels help you choose the way in which you want to engage with your customers. You might want certain mechanisms turned on or off.
  • Creating a new Funnel will give you the basic Javascript code that you can install on your website.
  • We encourage both Web and Mobile Engagement for a complete customer experience.

Install Funnel Javascript

Creating a New Funnel will give you a simple Javascript code. Install it on your website.

A Sample is shown here below, but it is recommended you generate one using Funnel instead of using this.

<script type="text/javascript">
   var _visualrendezvousconfig = {
      apiKey: 'PROJECT_API_KEY_FROM_ADMIN_SCREEN',
      highlight: true,
      photo: true,
      speech2text: true,
      audio: true
      valign: 'bottom',
      halign: 'right',
      lang: 'en'
   }; 
   (function() {
	  var s = document.createElement('script');
	  s.type = 'text/javascript';
	  s.async = true;
	  s.src = '//js.visualrendezvous.com/vrendezvous.js';
	  var x = document.getElementsByTagName('head')[0];
	  x.appendChild(s);
   })();
</script>

Gather Customer Feedback

Now that you have installed the Javascript code on your website, you can expect your customers to send you feedback or report issues using the Visual Rendezvous tools. Customers and Users of your website can send rich feedback via Web or Mobile devices. All the feedback/issues you receive will be available on the Dashboard for you and you will be alerted according to your Notifications setup. You will have several means to respond or take action and you will have the same tools as your customers in case you have to respond to them. From our experience using visual or verbal means resolve issues much quicker.

Here are some ways in which your Customers/Users will send you feedback or report issues:

Highlights

Let your customers send you visual feedback on the web page they are on. They can highlight parts of the page and blackout any sensitive information. You will receive a snapshot of what your customers saw as an image. A support person can quickly resolve an issue if he has visual clues of what a customer is facing. Highlights are the best way to gather them.

We will keep improving highlights to provide you with the most relevant information and over a period of time automatically suggest you solutions.

Photo

It is very easy today to capture a picture on a mobile device or even a web browser (WebRTC). A picture conveys more meaning than a text blurb describing an issue. Let your customers send you pictures of their issues or give you visual feedback. Your support team will be able to respond much better and quicker with visual clues.

You can use this Photo feature not just for issue reporting, but also in other creative ways. Crowdsource images about your brand - go social.

Speech to Text

This is an experimental feature using Google's Speech API. It is just a mere convenience for your customers.

Audio

Let your customers send you audio messages. Think of it as a voicemail. It is easy for someone to describe the problem they are facing than typing a long message about it. You don't have to let your customer reach you via phone or be on hold. Let them tell you their problem sooner.

Don't just stop at receiving audio messages from the customer, respond to them with it! It is like an asynchronous voice conversation and can be done at your convenience as you see fit. Our service will notify all parties involved everytime there is a new message.

Video

Allow your customers to go visual! We are working on this at the moment, and are keeping a close track on WebRTC to evolve to implement a non-flash solution.

Screen Recording

Your customer have an issue? Let them record what they are seeing and send it to you without having to install applications. We are working on this at the moment, and are keeping a close track on WebRTC to evolve to implement a non-flash solution.

Dashboard and Administration

Our Dashboard provides you with an all encompassing Helpdesk platform. You can review and respond right from within the Dashboard from any device. All the high-resolution mechanisms are at your disposable to connect back with your customer.

A glimpse of things you can do from the Dashboard, including Administration:

  • Review, follow up, and act on every feedback/issue gather via any funnel element.
  • Setup Notifications on Administration page, in order to be alerted/notified as you wish. You can choose to be notified in several ways (email, sms...)
  • User management - Manage customer support personnel. Each get their own Dashboard. You can measure the overall status and even individual status./li>
  • Setup Rules - You think certain Funnels are more important. Get notified with varying degree of priority.
  • Learn from Analytics what is important to you and what your customers think are important.
